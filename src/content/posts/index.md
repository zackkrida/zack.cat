Hi there, I'm Zack.

I'm currently on sabbatical and exploring some personal projects. I am open to consulting and opportunities from those who already know how to reach me.

I'm also a maintainer of the [Mathesar](https://mathesar.org/) project where I served as Product and Community Lead.

Previously, I led a group of developers sponsored by [Automattic](https://automattic.com/) to work on [Openverse](/openverse), an open source search engine for openly-licensed media.

Before that, I wrote open source software at [Creative Commons](https://creativecommons.org); worked as a consultant on internal software for startups, agencies, and local businesses; and worked in the brutal world of digital agencies. I'll write more about all of that someday.
